“Evil doesn’t vanish… it only hides in code.”

I was preparing a special gift for everyone to celebrate this Halloween — something unique, something fun... But before I could finish, it was stolen by a shadowy group calling themselves APT. They tampered with it, twisted its insides, and left behind a cryptic program—something that whispers in the dark, something alive.

Now the code itself seems... cursed. Every attempt to retrieve my gift only leads deeper into the labyrinth they’ve left behind.

Can you reverse their creation, unravel its secrets, and help me recover what was once meant to bring joy — before Halloween night falls and it becomes something far more sinister?

👻 Beware… the gift might be watching you too.
